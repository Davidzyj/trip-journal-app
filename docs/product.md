# Product Brief

## Concept

Travel Memo App / 旅途记 is a private, offline travel journal for people who want one place to keep everything about a trip: what to bring, how they traveled, where they went, and what they felt.

## Audience

- Travelers who prefer a lightweight record instead of a social travel platform.
- Users who care about privacy and want trip memories stored locally.
- Bilingual Chinese/English users preparing for App Store release in multiple regions.

## Version 1.0.0

The first version focuses on local capture and review. It intentionally avoids accounts, maps APIs, cloud sync, social sharing, and AI features.

## Data Model

- Trip
- Packing item
- Transportation record
- Place visit
- Journal entry

## Positioning

An intimate travel notebook, not a trip booking app, route planner, or social feed.
