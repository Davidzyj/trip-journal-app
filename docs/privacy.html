Travel Memo App

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Travel Memo App is designed as an offline travel journal. The app does not require an account and does not send your travel records to our servers.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data, usage analytics, diagnostics, location data, photos, contacts, or identifiers through the app.

Local Storage

Trips, packing items, transportation records, visited places, and journal entries are stored locally on your iPhone. You can delete this data by deleting records inside the app or deleting the app from your device.

Network Access

The app itself does not make network requests. It may open this privacy policy or the support page in your browser when you tap those links.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact jay212315@gmail.com.
